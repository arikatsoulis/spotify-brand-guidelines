colour

Spotify Green is our hero colour. We use Spotify Green to clearly signify the presence of Spotify in people's lives, and to help our users immediately indentify us. Use this color generously for our type, notes, charts and as a background.

*Neutral Colouring

We have a select set of supporting neutral colours which help Spotify Green to sing. In the majority of uses, we want strong contrast between all of the colours used.
